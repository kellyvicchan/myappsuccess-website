Our Approach

Most students have the potential to be outstanding candidates, they just don't know it.

Advisors Who Have Been There

Our founders navigated the U.S. college admissions process alone with no available resources or family guidance. They went on to secure national scholarships (QuestBridge, Burger King, Rotary Club), earn degrees from Cornell and Johns Hopkins, and work at institutions such as Apple, Accenture, Adobe, Goldman Sachs, and Harvard.

We built MyAppSuccess so every student has access to the roadmap we had to piece together ourselves. Having tested our framework in private sessions leading to student admissions at 113 colleges, we are now sharing our strategy to empower many more.

With mentors from Harvard, Cornell, Johns Hopkins, MIT, Stanford, and UPenn with 22 years of combined experience, we guide students of all backgrounds through the college admissions process.

Other Programs Prep Applications, We Prep Candidates

What if we told you that you are more interesting and capable than you think? Our framework does more than help you navigate the college application process and make you sound good on paper. It is designed to actually build you up throughout the process and long after college.

The focus isn't just instruction, it's empowerment.

Elite Access, Real Guidance

Connections to mentors and admissions officers, paired with resources designed to craft your unique, individual story. Students will leave the bootcamp with knowledge of essential, real-world skills such as storytelling, personal branding, and self-awareness that serve them in the college admissions process and beyond. Our strategy is curated to help you build meaningful applications for every target school. Your results come from how you apply it. If you put in the work, you will leave feeling more confident, distinctive, and ready to tackle what's next.

The bootcamp is built for high school juniors, rising seniors, and seniors who need support fast. It also works well for international students, competitive program applicants, and anyone who wants to put their strongest application forward.
