Our Approach

Most students have the potential to be outstanding candidates, they just don't know it.

01

We Are Advisors Who Have Been There

Our founders navigated U.S. college admissions alone, with no resources or family guidance. They went on to win national scholarships (QuestBridge, Burger King, Rotary Club), earn degrees from Cornell and Johns Hopkins, and work at places like Apple, Accenture, Adobe, Goldman Sachs, and Harvard. We built MyAppSuccess so every student has access to the roadmap we had to piece together ourselves, tested through private sessions that have led to admissions at 113 colleges, with mentors bringing 22 years of combined experience from Harvard, Cornell, Johns Hopkins, MIT, Stanford, and UPenn.

02

Other Programs Prep Applications, We Prep Candidates

What if you're more interesting and capable than you think? Our framework does more than help you navigate the application process and sound good on paper; it's designed to build you up throughout the process and long after college. The focus isn't just instruction, it's empowerment.

03

Elite Access, Real Guidance

Connections to mentors and admissions officers, paired with resources to craft your unique story. You'll leave with real-world skills, storytelling, personal branding, self-awareness, that serve you in admissions and beyond. Our strategy helps you build meaningful applications for every target school. Put in the work, and you'll leave more confident, distinctive, and ready for what's next.
